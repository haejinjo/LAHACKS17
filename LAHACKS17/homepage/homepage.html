Meet Eidos

Our Values

We can solve the problem of stadium parking by eliminating the hassle of looking for a spot and quickly connecting homeowners to car drivers. We want to improve the experience of people going to different Olympic events by eliminating excess costs and struggles associated with parking and bring you peace of mind.

Our Vision

We guarantee 3 things: safety, affordability, and accessibility. We want to establish trust between the customer and the homeowner by creating a rating system that allows each party to review each others experience. We will provide additional homeowner background checks to ensure the customer's safety.

Our Team

The Los Angeles 2024 Olympics is an exciting upcoming opportunity for our team. We are dedicated and passionate to helping the community engage with this event.
